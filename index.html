About

Welcome; Willkommen; ようこそ! Passionate about higher education and technology, my background is an interconnected career path. Blending my executive; learning and software expertise, my objective is combining three interrelated disciplines; a) Project Management (implementation); b) Human Centric-Design (collaboration); and, c) Strategic Organizational Behavior (leadership) towards developing data-focused products; and, creating applications for both technical end-users and non-technical audiences in a remote-role as a part of a dynamic, global team.

Paying-it-forward: During the US Subprime Financial Crisis, transitioning from an IT Executive to Higher Ed availed me the opportunity to give back and weather this major economic downturn. With an MBA, as an undergraduate professor while teaching in Asia; concurrently, I earned a second master’s degree (Teaching), and, then, a Doctorate of Education, Organizational Change and Leadership (OCL) from the University of Southern California (USC). Researching and writing my dissertation expanded interests in data analysis; visualization; UX design and rekindled my (long-standing) desire to learn to code.

Full-Stack Application

Although positive, the highly-modularized nature of the JavaScript ecosystem—one file for one module, view, etc. can reduce an application's load time. Furthermore, files oftentimes contain more information than actually needed / required, e.g., to ensure they can more easily be understood by other developers), which can also further present performance challenges / issues. Hence a major reason why the build process is vital to establish correctly during the development process to ensure better performance, readability and code maintenance.

Built using Bootstrap; CSS; Express; HTML; JavaScript; MongoDB and Node.js; this website has a custom written API allowing end-users access to movie information as well as the ability to create, delete and/or edit their own unique accounts.
